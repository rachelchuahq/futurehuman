Our Quality Commitment

At Future Human, we believe quality is worth waiting for.

biotech

We don't keep products sitting on shelves. Once your order is confirmed, our manufacturing partner begins producing your lot, which is then independently verified before being released for shipment.

This approach allows us to provide products from freshly produced lots while maintaining strict quality standards every step of the way.

science

Independent Third-Party Testing

Before any lot is released, we send one vial to an independent third-party laboratory for verification.

Every lot is tested for:

✓ Identity
✓ Purity
✓ Peptide Content (Assay)
info

In addition, we perform expanded testing—including sterility and endotoxin testing—on a scheduled basis, when qualifying new suppliers, and whenever additional verification is required.

hourglass_empty

Why Shipping Takes a Little Longer

Because every order is produced specifically for you and independently tested before release, our processing times are longer than companies that keep inventory on the shelf.

We know waiting isn't always ideal, but we'd rather spend the extra time verifying quality than rush products out the door.

visibility

Quality Through Transparency

Every product is linked to a manufacturing lot and undergoes our quality review before shipment.

By producing in small quantities, independently verifying every lot, and maintaining complete traceability, we're committed to delivering research products with the consistency, quality, and transparency you deserve.
