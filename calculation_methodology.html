Calculation Methodology

The Future Human Peptide Calculator utilizes a deterministic mathematical framework to convert raw chemical quantities into actionable administration data. By standardizing the relationship between lyophilized mass, diluent volume, and target dosage, we provide a consistent baseline for clinical research interpretation.

Our algorithm performs multi-stage conversions based on three primary user inputs: total peptide mass (Vial Size), liquid volume used for reconstitution (Bacteriostatic Water/Saline), and the desired research dosage.

Mathematical Formulas

The following logic is executed sequentially to ensure precision at every step of the conversion process.

Step 01: Base Concentration
Concentration (mg/mL) = Peptide Amount (mg) / Liquid Volume (mL)
Step 02: Unit Conversion
Concentration (mcg/mL) = Concentration (mg/mL) × 1,000
Step 03: Delivery Volume
Required Volume (mL) = Desired Amount (mcg) / Concentration (mcg/mL)
Step 04: Hardware Calibration
Syringe Units (U-100) = Required Volume (mL) × 100

Note: Formula assumes standard U-100 insulin syringe (1mL = 100 Units).
